Lover's Scratch: Couple Game

Puuoo Apps ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Lover's Scratch mobile application (the "App") for Android and iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Anonymous Account

The App uses Firebase Authentication with anonymous sign-in. A randomly generated user ID (UID) is created when you first open the App. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information during this process.

Optional Account Linking

If you choose to enable account recovery, you may link your anonymous account using:

  • Google Sign-In (Android): We receive your email address and authentication tokens.
  • Apple Sign-In (iOS): We receive your email address (which may be a private relay address) and authentication tokens.

Account linking is entirely optional and is used solely for account recovery purposes.

Game Data

We store the following data in Cloud Firestore, linked to your anonymous UID:

  • Scratch card results and prize history
  • Prize settings and wishlist preferences (emoji symbols, descriptions, rarity values)
  • Custom sticker selections and their URLs
  • Partnership data: invite codes, partner connections, streak counts, and scratch availability timestamps
  • Board game progress (positions, dice rolls, level)
  • Premium subscription status

Local Device Data

The App stores preferences locally on your device (via SharedPreferences), including onboarding status, notification settings, theme preferences, and cached partner data. This data does not leave your device.

Device Information

We collect limited device information for app functionality:

  • Timezone: To schedule local notifications at appropriate times
  • Locale/Language: To display the App in your preferred language (Portuguese, English, Spanish, or German)
  • Screen dimensions: To adapt the interface to your device
  • Operating system: To provide platform-specific features

Advertising Data

The App displays ads through Google AdMob. When ads are shown, Google may collect device information, advertising identifiers, and ad interaction data. On iOS, the App requests your consent via App Tracking Transparency (ATT) before collecting data used for ad personalization.

Purchase Data

If you make an in-app purchase (premium ad-free upgrade), the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not collect or store payment information such as credit card numbers or billing addresses.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the scratch card game experience and store your game progress
  • To enable multiplayer partnerships and streak tracking between partners
  • To connect you with your partner via invite codes
  • To schedule local notifications when new scratch cards are available
  • To display rewarded video advertisements (for free-tier users)
  • To process your premium purchase status (ad removal)
  • To enable account recovery if you link a Google or Apple account

4. Advertising & Tracking

The App shows rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. You can watch ads to unlock gameplay features such as additional scratch cards and dice rolls.

  • On iOS, we request your permission via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before any tracking for ad personalization. You can change this at any time in your device Settings.
  • On iOS, Apple's SKAdNetwork is used for ad attribution without identifying you personally.
  • On Android, Google's advertising identifier may be used for ad personalization.

You can remove all ads by purchasing the premium upgrade within the App. For more details, see How Google Uses Information.

5. In-App Purchases

The App offers a premium upgrade to remove advertisements. All payment processing is handled entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment details.

6. Notifications

The App uses local notifications only to remind you when your daily scratch card is available, when you have unused dice rolls, or as evening reminders. These notifications are scheduled on your device and are not sent from external servers. You can disable notifications at any time through your device's system settings.

7. Data Storage & Security

Game data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, which provides:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Security rules that restrict access to authorized users
  • Data centers compliant with industry-standard certifications

Local data stored on your device (SharedPreferences) is not encrypted by default and could be accessible if your device is compromised. We recommend keeping your device secured with a passcode or biometric lock.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your partner: Prize configurations, scratch activity, streak data, and board game state are shared with the partner you connect with through the App.
  • With service providers: Firebase (database hosting), Google AdMob (ad serving), and Klipy/GIPHY (sticker search) as described in Section 3.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is processed and stored via Google Firebase, which may use servers located in the United States and the European Union. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations. Google provides adequate safeguards for data transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including Standard Contractual Clauses for transfers outside the EU/EEA.

10. Data Retention & Deletion

Game data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete all your data at any time through the App's Settings page. When you request deletion:

  • Your partnership document and all associated data are permanently removed
  • Your prize history is deleted
  • Your custom sticker configurations are deleted
  • Your shared board game data is removed
  • Your partner's connection to your account is severed
  • Your Firebase authentication account is deleted
  • All local data on your device is cleared

This deletion is immediate and irreversible. You may also contact us at puuoosoftware@gmail.com to request data deletion.

11. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (also available directly in the App).
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we process your data.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at puuoosoftware@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Portugal, this is the Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados (CNPD).

12. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the App uses anonymous authentication, no personally identifiable information is collected from any user by default. If you believe a child under 13 has used the App, please contact us at puuoosoftware@gmail.com.
