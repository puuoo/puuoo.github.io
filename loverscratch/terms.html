2. Description of Service

Lover's Scratch is a couples' daily scratch card game designed for partners to play together. The App provides the following features:

  • Partner Connection: Connect with your partner via a unique invite code.
  • Prize Configuration: Each partner creates at least 7 prizes for the other, with custom emoji symbols and descriptions.
  • Daily Scratch Cards: One scratch card per day with a chance to win prizes your partner has set for you.
  • Streak System: Track consecutive days both partners scratch their cards.
  • Board Game: A shared Snakes & Ladders board game where scratching earns dice rolls.
  • Custom Stickers: Replace default emoji symbols with stickers from the sticker library.
  • Premium Upgrade: An optional purchase to remove advertisements.
