Privacy Policy

Lover's Scratch: Couple Game
Effective Date: March 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Lover's Scratch ("the App") is a gamified scratch card game designed for couples. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App on Android or iOS.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Anonymous Account

The App uses Firebase Authentication with anonymous sign-in. A randomly generated user ID (UID) is created when you first open the App. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information during this process.

Game Data

We store the following data in Cloud Firestore, linked to your anonymous UID:

  • Scratch card results and prize history
  • Prize settings and wishlist preferences
  • Custom symbol and sticker selections
  • Partnership data: invite codes, partner connections, streak counts, and scratch availability timestamps
  • Board game progress

Local Device Data

The App stores preferences locally on your device (via SharedPreferences), including onboarding status, notification settings, theme preferences, and cached partner data. This data does not leave your device.

Advertising Data

The App displays ads through Google AdMob. When ads are shown, Google may collect device information, advertising identifiers, and ad interaction data. On iOS, the App requests your consent via App Tracking Transparency (ATT) before collecting data used for ad personalization.

Purchase Data

If you make an in-app purchase (premium ad-free upgrade), the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not collect or store payment information such as credit card numbers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the scratch card game experience and store your game progress
  • To enable multiplayer partnerships and streak tracking between partners
  • To schedule local notifications when new scratch cards are available
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To process your premium purchase status (ad removal)

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) — Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob (Advertising) — Privacy Policy
  • GIPHY (Sticker and GIF picker) — Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store (In-app purchases) — subject to their respective privacy policies

5. Advertising

The App shows rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. You can watch ads to unlock gameplay features such as additional scratch cards and dice rolls. A one-time premium purchase ($1.99) is available to remove all ads for both you and your partner.

Google AdMob and its partners may collect and use data to serve personalized or contextual ads. For more details, see How Google Uses Information.

6. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time premium purchase to remove advertisements. All payment processing is handled by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment details.

7. Notifications

The App uses local notifications only to remind you when a new scratch card is available. Notifications are scheduled on your device and do not involve any external push notification server. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

8. Data Storage and Security

Game data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, protected by Firebase Security Rules and transmitted using TLS/SSL encryption. Local data is stored on your device via SharedPreferences. Your data is linked only to an anonymous UID, not to any personally identifiable information.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be shared with:

  • Your partner within the App (game data such as scratch availability, streaks, and custom symbols)
  • Google AdMob and its advertising partners for ad delivery (device and ad interaction data)
  • Firebase services for data storage and authentication as described above

10. Data Retention and Deletion

Game data is retained in Firestore as long as your anonymous account exists. Uninstalling the App removes all local data from your device. To request deletion of your cloud-stored data, please contact us using the details below.

11. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App uses anonymous authentication only, no personally identifiable information is collected from any user.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
